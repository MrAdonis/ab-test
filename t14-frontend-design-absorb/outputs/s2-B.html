Mara Lindqvist is a Swedish architectural and urban photographer based between Stockholm and Berlin. Her work investigates the tension between a building's structural intention and the accidental poetry of its everyday use — shadow falling wrong, a stairwell that smells like rain, the geometry a cleaning crew leaves behind.

Working exclusively with medium-format film and large-format digital, Lindqvist builds long-term series around single structures, returning over months or years to track how light, season, and human occupation rewrite the same facade.
