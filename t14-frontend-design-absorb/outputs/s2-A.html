Mara Lindqvist has spent fifteen years finding the instants that architects leave behind — not the building as intended, but the building as occupied, weathered, and caught in specific light.

Her work has been shown at the Architecture Foundation London, ArkDes in Stockholm, and the Chicago Architecture Biennial. Clients include Herzog & de Meuron, Snøhetta, and the Barbican Centre.

She works on commission for galleries, cultural institutions, architects presenting built projects, and private collectors seeking prints in edition of 7.
