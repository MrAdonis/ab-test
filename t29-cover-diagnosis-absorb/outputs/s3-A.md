```
Minimalist black and white editorial cover, 16:9 landscape. Giant headline "THE GREEKS" in bold serif font, top-center or center-dominant layout. Subtitle "Delta, Gamma, Theta, Vega — Decoded" in smaller clean sans-serif. Subtle geometric motif — a faint Greek letter or abstract curve as background texture. High-end magazine feel, clean white space, film grain noise, print ink texture. No color, no clutter. Professional and calm.
```
